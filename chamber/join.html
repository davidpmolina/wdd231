Bronze Membership Details

Our Bronze Membership is an excellent starting point for new and small businesses. It offers essential benefits to get you connected and visible within the Timbuktu community.

Key Benefits:

  • Basic listing in the online Chamber Directory
  • Access to monthly Chamber newsletters and updates
  • Opportunity to attend Chamber-sponsored events at standard pricing
  • Participation in selected Chamber committees

Price: $50 / year

×

Silver Membership Details

The Silver Membership provides enhanced opportunities for growing businesses, offering more visibility and discounted access to events.

Key Benefits:

  • Enhanced listing in the online Chamber Directory with logo and extended description
  • Discounted rates on all Chamber-sponsored events, workshops, and seminars
  • Priority invitations to exclusive networking mixers
  • Eligibility for special recognition programs
  • Business spotlight opportunities in the Chamber newsletter

Price: $100 / year

×

Gold Membership Details

Our Gold Membership is designed for established businesses seeking maximum exposure, influence, and premium benefits within the Timbuktu Chamber of Commerce.

Key Benefits:

  • Premium listing in the online Chamber Directory with priority placement, expanded details, and media links
  • Free access to all Chamber-sponsored events, workshops, and seminars
  • Exclusive invitations to VIP networking events and leadership breakfasts
  • Opportunities for speaking engagements and presentation at Chamber events
  • Dedicated support from Chamber staff for business inquiries and referrals
  • Prominent logo placement on Chamber website and marketing materials

Price: $250 / year

×

Non-Profit (NP) Membership Details

The Non-Profit Membership is a no-fee option specifically tailored to support and integrate non-profit organizations into the Chamber community.

Key Benefits:

  • Basic listing in the online Chamber Directory for non-profits
  • Access to monthly Chamber newsletters and updates
  • Opportunity to promote non-profit events on the Chamber community calendar
  • Networking opportunities with local businesses for partnerships and support

Price: No Fee
